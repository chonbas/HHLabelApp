Welcome to the #HackHarassment Labeler App

Instructions:

  1. Click on "Label"" in the navigation bar
  2. You will see a screen showing two buttons: "Not Harassment" and "Harassment" as well as a text comment
  3. Read the comment and decide how to label the comment.
  4. Do not over-think the label, if you have any doubts default to "Not Harassment"
  5. For ease of use, you can utilize the'-' and '=' keys at the end of the number bar atop your keyboard to label. The '-' key corresponds to 'Not Harassment', the '=' key correponds to 'Harassment'.
